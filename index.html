Former Dancer, Future Developer

First of all, welcome to my personal Portfolio. This is my first attempt at creating a pure coded Portfolio, and the purpose of this webpage is to introduce myself and to showcase some of my work and side projects that are software related.


My name is Joe Bentley, I am 27 years and I recently career changed from Dancing in hope to find employment in the tech industry. Dancing was always my passion from a young age it took me all over the world, Dancing in many countries and performing on cruise ships. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, I took the lockdown period to self teach some new skills and I discovered a new passion - Software Development.


I have recently graduated from an intensive bootcamp: Master Code course with Code Nation. A fully remote learning course, which taught me many new tech and social skills within the tech industry.


Despite Dancing and Developing, I am an enthusiastic Welsh Evertonian, with a avid passion for video games and sports.
